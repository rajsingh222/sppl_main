Overview

The Digitex system offers advanced monitoring capabilities for structures, using cutting-edge software to gather and analyze data from multiple xDAS units in real-time. With its ability to quickly distribute alerts and data to remote locations over the internet, the system ensures that response measures can be taken quickly in the event of an emergency, such as an earthquake.

With its “quick analysis” feature, the Digitex system can also provide valuable insights into the interaction between dynamic loads and a structure’s modal characteristics, making it an essential tool for engineers and researchers. The system is versatile, working with multiple types of units and sensor types, and comes with Digitex D-Series accelerometers for advanced analysis.

Key Features

  • Flexibility in IoT integration and expansion options
  • Wireless or low-cost cable communication options
  • No noise in cables due to digitalization at sensor location
  • Low power consumption and POE capability
  • Automated PDF reporting and easy maintenance/replacement
  • Scalability with up to 256 units and parallel networks
  • Low cost on-site installation
  • Multi-sensor compatibility
