About Us

An IIT Delhi incubated company with leading expertise in technical operations and research

At Sanrachna Prahari Pvt. Ltd, we are pioneering the future of Health Monitoring (SHM) in India. Specializing in cutting-edge technologies for assessing and maintaining the integrity of all civil infrastructure, especially bridges and buildings, we offer comprehensive solutions designed to ensure the longevity and safety of critical structures. From high-rise buildings and heritage monuments to industrial plants and transportation networks, our mission is to revolutionize infrastructure management through innovation, research, and expert training. Incubated in Foundation for Innovation & Technology Transfer (FITT), IIT Delhi, Sanrachna Prahari has distinguished faculty of IIT Delhi, as part of the core team.
