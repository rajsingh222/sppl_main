Overview

This system offers advanced monitoring capabilities for structures, using cutting-edge software to gather and analyze data from multiple xDAS units in real-time. With its ability to quickly distribute alerts and data to remote locations over the internet, the system ensures that response measures can be taken quickly in the event of an emergency, such as an earthquake.

With its “quick analysis” feature, this system can also provide valuable insights into the interaction between dynamic loads and a structure’s modal characteristics, making it an essential tool for engineers and researchers. The system is versatile, working with multiple types of units and sensor types, and comes with D-Series accelerometers for advanced analysis.

Key Features

  • Flexibility in IoT integration and expansion options
  • Wireless or low-cost cable communication options
  • No noise in cables due to digitalization at sensor location
  • Low power consumption and POE capability
  • Automated PDF reporting and easy maintenance/replacement
  • Scalability with up to 256 units and parallel networks
  • Low cost on-site installation
  • Multi-sensor compatibility

Overview

This xDAS AVM System is the perfect solution for monitoring vibrations in structures.

With its portable, battery-operated design and wireless capabilities, it’s easy to set up and use in any location. The system offers a variety of options, including the ability to extend wireless coverage and support for multiple sensor types. The xDAS AVM System is designed to run unattended for extended periods of time, making it ideal for long-term monitoring projects. Overview

This SHM system offers a cost-effective solution for monitoring any structure, from single-story homes to multi-story buildings and unique structures. Our advanced accelerometer technology and cloud-based data aggregation, communication, and sensing capabilities provide unparalleled reliability and accuracy. With our system, you can easily monitor the health of your structure with one or more Pulse units connected to our cloud-based Voyager data acquisition and processing software.

Key Features

  • Build your SHM system with Pulse units for real-time streaming and various recording options (triggered, scheduled, ring-buffer, manual).
  • Access your data remotely with cloud-based data acquisition and processing software (Voyager®) and compatibility with Observer® and OMA Suite.
  • Wireless connectivity with DigiMESH® and DigiSync® for time and data synchronization.
  • High-quality AD conversion with 20-bit resolution and embedded 3-channel accelerometer with low noise level.
  • User-selectable accelerometer range and sampling rate.
  • Power options including Micro-USB port, POE, and battery backup for easy installation.
  • Secure web access to your system.
  • Compatibility with other software such as Observer® and OMA Suite for data analysis and management.
