Vision and Mission

SPPL Vision and Mission

At Sanrachna Prahari, our vision is to become the trusted partner for the nation in ensuring the safety, reliability, and sustainability of the civil infrastructure in India. Through innovative research, technical expertise, products and services. we are committed to shaping a future where infrastructure assets are managed efficiently and maintained to the highest standards.


Vision Statement

“Ensuring safety, longevity, and sustainability of infrastructure through cutting-edge technology, innovative research, and world-class services in Structural Health Monitoring.”


Mission Statement

“To deliver advanced Structural Health Monitoring solutions using IoT based, artificial neural network supported, sensor technology, that will empower a nation to protect and enhance the safety and performance of their infrastructure, through expert consultation and uncompromising commitment to quality.”


Aim

“To revolutionise the way infrastructure is monitored, maintained, and managed, ensuring resilience and sustainability for generations to come.”
